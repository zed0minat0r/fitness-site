# Iron & Flow Fitness — Website Vision

## Purpose
Boutique fitness studio combining strength training and mobility/yoga. Personal trainers, class schedules, membership pricing, transformation results.

## Design
- Dark energetic palette: deep charcoal #1a1a1a, electric orange #FF6B35, clean white #F5F5F5
- Bold sans-serif typography (Inter or Montserrat)
- Mobile-first — gym-goers search on phones
- Should make someone want to sign up immediately
- Energetic but clean, not cluttered
